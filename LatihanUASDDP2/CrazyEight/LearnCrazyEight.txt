Peraturan Permainan Crazy Eight :
1. Setiap objek player dikasih 5 kartu atau lebih
2. dan ada satu kartu faced up di "discard pile"
3. sisa kartu lain di taroh di draw pile
4. setiap player takes turn to place a card from their pile on the discard pile if their catch matches the rank suit of the very top card, or if they want to place a wildcard
5. kalau gk ada dari kartu mereka yang match the card, they have to draw a card from the draw pile until they find an according one that matches
6. if the player cannot play because the stock pile is exhausted, the player must past the turn to the player on the left 
7. the game ended when a player card is empty
8. the remaining players got penalty according to their remaining cards, 8 is minus 20 points, face cards are worth 10, other cards are worth their ranks 

Kalau ada babak
1. the player with the lowest score when a player points hit 100 is the winner, or 50 x number of players

Facts
1. An eight cannot be the card to start a discard pile, if that happens shuffle the eight into the draw pile, and then draw a card again fron th very top of the draw pile 
2. kalau draw pile abis maka the player with the lowest score win
